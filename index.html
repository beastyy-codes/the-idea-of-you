The Idea of You

OH, this rain reminds me of you—
The beauty, the charm, the gleamy eyes of you.
Your wonderful smile never fails to mesmerize me;
I still love the idea of you.

I don't know what went wrong between us.
how this green meadow got reduced to dust.
They say true love hurts the most,
but I still hold on to the idea of you.

I cherish everything we shared—
the madness, the passion, the crazy us.
We stood strong no matter what storms came—
another reason I love the idea of us.

Hurting you was my worst nightmare,
I always dreamed of kissing you forever.
You're the one I cared for the most,
And I failed to make you feel loved forever.

I stood before the storm, fully armed,
taking the blows so you could stay unharmed.
Yet an old heatwave struck us hardest of all
overshadowing everything that once charmed.

You fell in love with a wanderer—
a boy with flaws, lost and unsure.
Your love transformed him into a man
full of values, full of hope—and you,
became the one he wanted beside him for life.

I vowed to treat you like a queen, and I stand by it—
I have—still vow to do all the things you love.
You still matter most to me, darling.
You've called me your 'man,' and I'll stand by it.

You're the answer to my prayers I never expected;
You've loved me like a mother, which I've always respected.
You gave your heart in your own man's hand,
And It got scratched in the way it was least expected.

Since you came into my life, I tried to love you endlessly,
Holding you close, giving all I had—
But old shadows found their way between us,
And you were wounded by ghosts I couldn’t hold back.

You're the one who glowed like chrome—
You're my calm, my comfort, my honeycomb.
We were always meant to be, and I hope you forgive me,
Because you, my baby... I still call you my home.

Oh, I still love the idea of you—
The laugh, the presence, the scent of you.
I want to love you and experience 'us'
like there's no tomorrow.
Oh, Shree—this rain reminds me of you.
